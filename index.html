White Lies is an NPR investigative podcast that revisits the 1965 murder of Rev. James Reeb, a minister who traveled to Selma, Alabama, during the civil rights movement. The series exposes cover-ups, hidden truths, and the legacy of racism in America, piecing together why the case was left unsolved for decades.
